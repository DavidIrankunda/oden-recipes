Citrus Cake

Tri-colored cake with a lemon layer, lime layer and an orange layer. The filling is a lemon curd and the frosting is orange.
Recipe by HILARY2000 Updated on July 14, 2022

Fewer colors? Yes. Less flavor? No way!
This gorgeous rainbow citrus cake features
three different colors, each representing
a different citrus fruit — oranges, lemons, and limes.
If you love zesty cakes, this recipe is for you!
