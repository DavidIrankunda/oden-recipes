Rainbow Cupcakes

Let's begin with cupcakes —
perhaps the easiest rainbow cake option of all.
These top-rated cupcakes feature four colors,
but you can do six if you prefer. Either way, bask in all the glory,
closely guarding the secret that they were
actually easy to make! As reviewer zebracupcake15 says, "Really fun recipe...
everyone thinks it's complicated but it really isn't!"
